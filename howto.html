What do to next

If it's got a `$` in front, do it in your terminal.

  1. $ echo "5. Start writing your new app!" >> howto.html
  2. sed '34 a <li> Deploy a new app to drie </li> ' howto.html
  3. $ git checkout -b $YOUR_NAME-feature
  4. $ git commit -am "Testing a change on drie"
  5. Your choice:
    1. a. $ curl $YOUR_NAME-feature.drie-starter-app.next.drie.co
    2. b. Go to $YOUR_NAME-feature.drie-starter-app.next.drie.co in your browser.
